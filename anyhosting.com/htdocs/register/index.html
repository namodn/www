Domain Name Availability

An Internet domain is critical to your web site. After all , it's your Internet "address". It should be your business name, a variation of your business name, or it should be related to your main product or service.
 
    There are three main, top level, public domains:
  • .com
  • .net
  • .org
When you check a domain, be sure to check the .com and .net variations. You can also use .org if your organization qualif ies as non-profit. Only educational and government agencies can register .edu and .gov domains.
