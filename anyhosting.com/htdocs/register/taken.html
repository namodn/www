Don't give up just yet!  


Having your own domain name is an essential tool to successful Internet marketing.     There are several variations of domain names that may work for your business.

The following tips may aid in your search:
  • Try both .com and .net as an extension
  • Try adding a "-" in your domain name
  • Try an associated product as opposed to your company name
  • Try a business slogan (ie. wedoitright.com)
  • Last but not least, use a thesaurus to try similar words and/or phrases
