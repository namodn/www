Check on a Domain

What you need to know:

    An Internet domain is critical to your web site, after all, it's your Internet "address".   It should be your business name, a variation of your business name, or it should be related to your main product or service.

There are five main top level domains:

  • .com
  • .net
  • .org
  • .edu
  • .gov
    When you check a domain, be sure to check the .com and .net variations.  You can also use .org if your organization qualifies as non-profit.  Only educational and government agencies can register .edu and .gov domain extensions.
