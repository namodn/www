About Namodn

ArtWritingMusic.com

Namodn is a small, not-for-profit orginazation dedicated to art in all it's forms (Art, Writing, Music, etc.). Our goal is to incorporate the many aspects of art into a business that can be of great use to artists of all kinds. We give people the opportunity to express themselves in a global medium. What people express is up to them; we will not censor other people's art.

List of services:

*We provide a place for artists, writers & musicians to get their art on the web for little or no money. From there people from all around the world can view art, read poetry & short stories, or download and listen to music. If you want to put your stuff up for people to look at free of charge (i.e. not trying to sell it), you will not be charged for the web page. If you would like to sell your art, music etc. you must pay a $25 setup fee, and a flat rate of $10 per. month (or $30 per. quarter). The content can be sold through the mail (i.e. paintings, sculpture, drawings, compact discs/tapes), or downloaded (mp3's, etc).

NOTICE TO ARTISTS : We do provide ftp access so you can make changes to your site. If, however, you would like us to do the web work for you, or other work such as scanning, recording, cd copying of your material, etc., we will charge a fee to cover operating costs.
